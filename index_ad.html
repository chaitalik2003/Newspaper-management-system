Introduction

The Newspaper Agency Management System is a digital platform designed to modernize newspaper delivery services by integrating customer, paper hawker, and admin operations into a cohesive system.

Customer Module:

Customers can easily register, select subscription plans (monthly, weekly, or yearly), and choose a paper hawker based on their area. Once a selection is made, the chosen paper hawker receives an email with the customer's details, including subscription start date, address, and contact information.

Know More →

Paper Hawker Module:

Paper hawkers receive notifications with customer details and can access an organized view of their assigned customers. This module helps paper hawkers plan delivery routes effectively, ensuring timely newspaper deliveries and improving communication with customers.

Know More →

Admin Module:

The admin oversees the entire system, managing customer registrations, subscriptions, and paper hawker listings. This module ensures smooth operations, maintains service quality, and provides tools for generating reports and analytics to improve decision-making.

Know More →

SCOPE OF THE PROJECT

-> Development and implementation of a comprehensive system for customer registrations, subscription management, and paper hawker assignments.
-> Facilitation of seamless communication between customers and paper hawkers.
